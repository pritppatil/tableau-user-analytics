### Best Posting Time

* Engagement fluctuates throughout the day, with noticeable peaks during **early and late posting hours**.
* Posting during low-engagement hours consistently results in weaker performance.

### Best Posting Day

* **Tuesday and Friday** show the highest average engagement.
* Monday and Wednesday perform relatively lower.
* Scheduling high-value content on peak days can improve reach and interaction.
